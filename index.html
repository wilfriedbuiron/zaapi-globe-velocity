01
Use Case 1

Brave Connective
as Zaapi Reseller

Brave Connective's M360 is the leading CPaaS in the Philippines — but it doesn't offer marketplace integrations or AI-powered full automation. Zaapi fills both gaps.

  • Resell Zaapi to M360 clients needing Shopee/Lazada/TikTok chat
  • White-label or co-branded AI automation suite
  • Expand Brave's portfolio into conversational commerce
  • Revenue share model aligned with Globe's platform strategy
Globe Context

Brave Connective (formerly AdSpark) recently partnered with Plug & Play and joined Bridge Alliance — signaling aggressive expansion into regional digital ecosystems. Zaapi's marketplace AI complements rather than competes with M360's messaging infrastructure.

02
Use Case 2

Automating Pre-Sales
for Ayala Land & Ayala Health

Large-scale click-to-chat campaigns on Meta generate massive lead volumes — but manual qualification is slow, costly, and inconsistent. Zaapi automates this entirely.

  • AI qualifies leads instantly via WhatsApp/Messenger
  • Auto-book property viewings & medical appointments
  • Filter high-intent buyers from tire-kickers at scale
  • Integrate with CRM for seamless handoff to sales teams
Why This Matters

Ayala Land just launched "Access Ayala Land" — a digital platform across its four residential brands reaching for 50M customers by end-2026. Pre-sales volumes will be enormous. Zaapi can serve as the AI front door for every property inquiry.

03
Use Case 3

Centralising Comms for
Ayala Hospitality

Seda Hotels and Ayala Land Hospitality operate properties across Luzon, Visayas and Mindanao — each with its own set of social accounts, email inboxes and messaging channels.

  • Centralise all property communications in one dashboard
  • AI handles booking inquiries, check-in info & concierge FAQs
  • Consistent guest experience across every Seda property
  • Multilingual support (English, Filipino, Mandarin)
Hospitality Context

Hotel groups manage distinct FB, IG, WhatsApp, TikTok and email accounts per property. This gets chaotic fast. Zaapi already helps hospitality groups in the region unify and automate all guest communications from a single pane of glass.
